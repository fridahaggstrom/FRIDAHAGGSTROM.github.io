ABOUT

This project is about creating a new typographic character that could translate the word silence. During my research I discovered that silence can mean a lot of things, both in philosophical and physical ways. I ended up asking myself what silence actually is. Does silence even exist?
As it turns out, the human ear can hear frequencies starting at 20Hz. I came to the conclusion that without sound there is no silence, and vice versa. The character I created is therefore sketched out of sound waves meeting each other at the lowest point. This is a sign for silence.
